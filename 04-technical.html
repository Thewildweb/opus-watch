CALIBRE OP-04 / NOTES FROM THE BENCH

An automatic three-hander,
finished by hand.

OP-04 is a modified ETA 2892-A2 base, raised to chronometer tolerances and re-finished entirely in our Antwerp workshop. The base plate carries an applied Côtes de Genève; bridges are bevelled to a 45° angle and polished by hand, edge by edge. The free-sprung balance runs at 28,800 vph and delivers a 72-hour reserve.

We regulate each calibre in six positions before casing — a certified dossier with rate, amplitude and beat error accompanies every watch. The dossier is signed by the regulator and dated to the day of casing.
