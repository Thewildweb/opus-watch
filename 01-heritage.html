A House of Watchmakers

The hours, unhurried, since 1924.

Four generations have set their hands to the same workbench in our atelier beneath the Rue des Granges. We make few watches, by intention. Each is numbered, signed, and recorded by the maker who completed it — a small act of bookkeeping that, in our trade, we still consider a kind of honesty.

Every Opus is welcomed home for service — and so are timepieces of other houses, vintage and rare. Our master watchmakers in Geneva accept work by appointment, and by post under our courier seal.

Full revision 5–7 weeks
Polishing & refinish 3 weeks
Crystal & gasket 10 days
Strap fitting same day
Movement restoration upon estimate

Pour vous écouter,
nous prenons rendez-vous.

Bring the watch in person, or send it under our insured courier — either way, your piece is opened, photographed, and reviewed before any work is quoted. You will hear back, by hand, within four working days.
