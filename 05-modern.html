— Calibre OP-04

Movement,
finished by hand
at the bench.

The OP-04 is our most refined movement to date: a free-sprung balance, a 72-hour reserve and chronometer-grade tolerances in six positions. Bridges are bevelled to a 45° angle and polished by hand.

Every calibre is regulated, cased and signed by the watchmaker who completes it, and ships with a dated rate dossier.
